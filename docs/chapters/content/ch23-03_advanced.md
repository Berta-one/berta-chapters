## Key concepts

- **Retention curve** — fraction of a cohort still active over time.
- **Flattening curve** — the sign of real product-market fit.
- **Funnel** — sequential conversion from visit to paying user.
- **Biggest leak** — fix the worst step first for the most impact.

Retention curves reveal whether users stick; funnels reveal where they leave. Flattening retention and patching the worst funnel step are the two highest-leverage growth moves.
